About Us

A modern English School for studying Conversational English as fast as possible. We offer practical speaking classes with experienced specialists. Our tutors are carefully selected. Before starting working with us, they pass multiple interviews and tests. After being selected, they get additional training from our school.

Who is it for

It is for people who are tired of traditional methods of teaching and are seeking something fun and interactive yet greatly useful. If you want to communicate with natives and non-natives without any problem, understand movies and songs better annnnd you already understand English a little, read on!!!

Our Courses

Conversational Movies Personalized

Conversational English

Are you tired of learning new words and grammar rules but still not being able to speak?

If yes, you are in the right place. While teaching conversational English, we focus on speaking skills. We teach our students to think in English with the help of different activities and exercises.

English with movies

If you loooove watching movies as we do, let's learn English with the help of movies. For each class, you and your teacher will choose a movie and discuss it. Along with discussing characters and scenes, you will also talk about words and phrases that are useful and interesting in that movie.

Special for you

Tell us what exactly you want to learn. If you have a particular topic that you would like to discuss or a particular skill to develop like listening, reading, grammar, etc., this course is perfect for you, as you get to decide what to learn and how to learn it.
